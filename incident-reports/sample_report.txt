WHAT HAPPENED
------------------------------------------------------------
While running my brute force simulation, Splunk started
throwing 4625 events faster than I expected. Hydra was
hammering the RDP port with rockyou.txt — 14 million
passwords queued up. Windows was logging every single
failed attempt in real time.

That's when the alert fired.

"Brute Force Detection" — the one I built earlier that day.
Seeing it trigger on an actual attack I launched myself
was honestly the most satisfying moment of this whole lab.

ANALYST NOTES

Biggest learning from this incident simulation:

any tuned SIEM would catch this immediately. Real attackers
who know what they're doing go slow and low to avoid
exactly this kind of detection.
